## 🎯 Quick Decision Framework

**Use AI Council when:**
- ✅ Multiple options exist
- ✅ Trade-offs are involved
- ✅ Stakes are meaningful
- ✅ You're unsure
- ✅ Security is involved
- ✅ Others are affected
- ✅ Long-term impact
- ✅ Complex problem

**Skip AI Council when:**
- ❌ Simple factual question
- ❌ Urgent/emergency
- ❌ You already decided
- ❌ Just need quick status
- ❌ Direct command

## 🦆 Remember

**AI Council is your multi-perspective thinking partner.**

Use it for:
- Big decisions
- Small decisions (if you want depth)
- Anything with trade-offs
- Anything with risks
- Anything important to you

**Don't use it for:**
- "What time is it?"
- "Is Palantir running?"
- Quick commands

**When in doubt, use Council!**
